XMPP, also known as the Extensible Messaging and Presence Protocol, is an open communication protocol designed for instant messaging (IM), presence information, and contact list maintenance. It enables near-real-time exchange of structured data between network entities and offers applications beyond traditional IM, including signaling for VoIP, video, file transfer, and more12.
