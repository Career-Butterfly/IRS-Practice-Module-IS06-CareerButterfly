Alibaba Cloud, also known as Aliyun, is a Chinese cloud computing company and a subsidiary of Alibaba Group. It provides cloud computing services to online businesses and Alibaba’s own e-commerce ecosystem. As the fourth-largest public cloud provider globally, it offers a wide range of services, including elastic compute, data storage, relational databases, big-data processing, anti-DDoS protection, and content delivery networks (CDN) 1.
