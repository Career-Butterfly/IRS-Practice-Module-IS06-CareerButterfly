The STM32WL series, developed by STMicroelectronics, integrates a general-purpose microcontroller and a sub-GHz radio on a single chip. These microcontrollers support multiple modulations, including LoRa, 2 (G)FSK, 4 (G)FSK, (G)MSK, OOK, and BPSK, making them ideal for long-range wireless applications with protocols like LoRaWAN, Sigfox, and more1.
