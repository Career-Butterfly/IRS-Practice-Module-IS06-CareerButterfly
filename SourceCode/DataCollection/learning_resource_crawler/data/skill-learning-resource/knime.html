KNIME, the Konstanz Information Miner, is a free and open-source data analytics, reporting, and integration platform. It empowers users to perform data analysis, manipulation, and transformation through a visual interface, making it a popular choice for data scientists and analysts who want to build and execute machine learning workflows1234.

Here are five free resources where you can learn more about KNIME:

  1. KNIME Learning Center: Explore various courses and validate your knowledge with industry-recognized certification.
  2. KNIME Press: Extend your knowledge with KNIME Press books and cheat sheets.
  3. KNIME Blog: Read articles about creating and productionizing data science.
  4. KNIME Forum: Visit the KNIME Forum to get answers to all your data science questions.
  5. KNIME Courses: Check out the official KNIME courses, and consider exploring platforms like Udemy and Coursera for additional KNIME courses56.
