In the tech world, a digital nomad is someone who travels freely while working remotely using technology and the internet. These individuals often have minimal material possessions and work from temporary housing, hotels, cafes, public libraries, co-working spaces, or recreational vehicles, relying on Wi-Fi, smartphones, or mobile hotspots to access the Internet123.
