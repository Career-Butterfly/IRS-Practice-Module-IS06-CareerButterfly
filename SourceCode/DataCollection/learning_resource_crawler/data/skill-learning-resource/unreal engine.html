Unreal Engine is a series of 3D computer graphics game engines developed by Epic Games, initially showcased in the 1998 first-person shooter video game Unreal. It has since been adopted across various genres of games and other industries, including film and television. Written in C++, it offers portability across desktop, mobile, console, and virtual reality platforms. The latest version, Unreal Engine 5, was launched in April 20221.
