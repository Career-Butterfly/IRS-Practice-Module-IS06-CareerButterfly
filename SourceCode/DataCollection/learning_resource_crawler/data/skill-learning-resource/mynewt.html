Apache Mynewt is a flexible and powerful platform for wireless IoT applications that supports various networking stacks, such as Bluetooth Low Energy 5, Bluetooth Mesh, Wi-Fi, LoRaWAN, and more. It offers security, management, operations, and cross-platform features for various microcontrollers and boards1.
