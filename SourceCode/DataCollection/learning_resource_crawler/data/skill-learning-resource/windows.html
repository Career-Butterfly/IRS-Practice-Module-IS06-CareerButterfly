Windows is the computer operating system from Microsoft. It has been around since 1985 and is pre-installed on many PCs. It allows you to interact with your computer, surf the web, play games, send emails, watch videos, process words, and more. With a market share of more than 60%, it is the most used desktop operating system in the world, ahead of macOS and Linux1.
