In the context of rosin extraction, Pillow Tech refers to the traditional method of pressing rosin, where rectangular filter bags are loaded with starting material and laid flat between the rosin press plates. This technique maximizes surface area during extraction and is commonly used for bubble hash or dry sift. For flower rosin, consider exploring Bottle Tech or Chottle Tech, which involve different orientations and bag configurations1.
