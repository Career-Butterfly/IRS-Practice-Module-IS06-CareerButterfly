A test lab refers to a controlled scientific environment, typically a laboratory or similar setting, where experiments and analyses are conducted to investigate various phenomena or validate hypotheses. In the context of medical testing, it specifically involves performing tests (such as blood tests or urinalysis) on patient specimens to assess health conditions123.
