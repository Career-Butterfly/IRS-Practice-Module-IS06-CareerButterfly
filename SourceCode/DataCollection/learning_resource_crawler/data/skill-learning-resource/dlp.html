Data Loss Prevention (DLP) is a security solution that identifies and helps prevent unsafe or inappropriate sharing, transfer, or use of sensitive data across on-premises systems, cloud-based locations, and endpoint devices1. It assists organizations in safeguarding sensitive information and achieving compliance with regulations such as HIPAA and GDPR.
